About

"Record Outlaw" is an exhilarating music adventure where players step into the shoes of a renegade DJ, navigating a dystopian society to uncover hidden tracks and defy the oppressive regime through the power of underground beats. With a gripping storyline, innovative gameplay mechanics, and a pulsating soundtrack, "Record Outlaw" promises an unforgettable journey through rhythm and rebellion.
